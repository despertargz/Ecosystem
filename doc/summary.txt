Rules
-------
Bear
    Maws lumberjack
    Moves 5 spaces
    If last lumberjack mawed, new one spawns

    0 maws in 12 months = +1 bear
    1+ maws in 12 months = -1 bear

Lumberjack
    Chops down tree(1 lumber)/elder tree(2 lumber))
    Moves 3 spaces

    After 12 months hire (# lumber / 10 rounded down) more
    If # of lumber < # of lumberjacks, let one go

Tree
    1 month = 10% chance for Tree to spawn adjacent sappling
    1 month = 20% chance for Elder tree to spawn sappling
    12 months = Sappling becomes Tree
    120 months = Tree becomes elder tree
